Healthy Living Tips

Physical Activity for Longevity

  • Aim for 150 minutes of moderate aerobic activity weekly.
  • Daily walking reduces current risk of stroke and heart disease.
  • Incorporate strength training twice a week for muscle health.
  • Activity improves mental clarity and reduces clinical brain fog.

Nutritional Balance & Vitality

  • Prioritize whole grains, lean proteins, and leafy greens.
  • Minimize processed sugars to maintain steady energy levels.
  • Hydrate with at least 8-10 glasses of clean water daily.
  • Antioxidant-rich foods support rapid cellular repair.

Mindfulness & Mental Peace

  • Practice deep breathing for 10 minutes every single day.
  • Engaging with nature significantly lowers baseline cortisol.
  • Maintain healthy work-life balance to avoid burnout risk.
  • Regular breaks improve overall cognitive focus and clarity.

Restorative Sleep & Recovery

  • Consistent 7-9 hours of sleep regulates immune function.
  • Avoid blue light from screens 1 hour before scheduled bed.
  • Maintain a dark, cool, quiet environment for deep rest.
  • Quality sleep is crucial for neuro-regenerative processes.
