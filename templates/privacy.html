Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 18, 2026

At easyMed, we take your medical privacy with the utmost seriousness. This policy describes how we collect, use, and handle your data on our multi-specialty telemedicine platform.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to provide medical care, including:

  • Personal identification (Name, Age, Gender).
  • Medical history and symptom descriptions.
  • Real-time vitals (Blood pressure, Heart rate, Oxygen levels).
  • Medical imaging (MRI/CT scans).

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively for clinical purposes:

  • Facilitating consultations between Rural Doctors and Specialists.
  • AI-driven risk assessment and condition prediction.
  • Generating clinical reports and analysis.

3. Data Security

All medical data is transmitted over secure HTTPS (SSL) connections and stored in encrypted databases. We do not share your medical information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Deletion

Users may request the deletion of their accounts and associated data at any time by contacting the platform administrator.

5. Compliance

easyMed aims to adhere to best practices for medical data handling and telemedicine security standards.
